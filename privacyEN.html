Privacy Policy

At Ducky Clicker, the privacy of our users is of utmost importance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile game application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information when you use our game, including:

  • Personal Information: Such as username, email address, or other contact details if you choose to register.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the game, including game statistics, levels completed, and time spent playing.
  • Device Data: Information about your mobile device, such as model, operating system, IP address, and performance data.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To improve and optimize the gaming experience.
  • To personalize in-game content.
  • To resolve technical issues and provide user support.
  • To send notifications or communications related to the game, such as updates or promotions.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following situations:

  • With service providers who help us operate and improve the game.
  • When necessary to comply with the law or respond to legal requests.
  • In case the game is acquired or merged with another company.

4. Security

We are committed to protecting your personal information. We use industry-standard security measures to keep your data safe. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security, as no system is completely infallible.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through an update in the application or other means. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed of any updates.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at antoniozu.dev@gmail.com or through the support section within the application.
